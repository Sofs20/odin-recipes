Chocolate Cookie

Description

A great snack to eat in the afternoon, in the company of a hot chocolate, a tea or a coffee.

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 1 cup of ferment
  • 1 chocolate tablet
  1. Preheat the oven to 180ÂºC.
  2. Beat the butter with the brown and powdered sugar to obtain a thick but creamy mass.
  3. Add the eggs, previously beaten, to the dough and mix well.
  4. Sift the flour and yeast into the mixture and mix again.
  5. After obtaining a homogeneous mass, break (with the help of a small knife) a chocolate bar into pieces of approximately 5 million and add to the mass.
  6. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and distribute spoonfuls of dough evenly.
  7. Take the tray out of the oven and let the cookies cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
